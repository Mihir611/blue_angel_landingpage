Lamborghini URUS

As most of us know the Lamborghini Urus is the second SUV made by Lamborghini\. Lamborghini took a giant leap in making a SUV to compete against their key rivals that is AUDI, BENTLEY\. The name “URUS” is named after a species of wild Ox that predates the modern domestic cattle, the Urus is Lamborghini’s second SUV that comes nearly 25 years after their first SUV, the LM002, went out of production\. It based on the same platform of that of the Porsche Cayenne, The Bentley Bentayga, and the Audi Q7\.

Now talking about the price, the Urus starts form about ₹3\.62 crores\. Yes, it is a pricey tag for and SUV but with high price comes high performance\. Coming to the performance of the car  
the Urus is powered by a 4\.0L Turbocharged V8 which puts out a monstrous 650 horsepower with 850NM of torque which is insane for an SUV\. This Italian SUV is capable of reaching form 0\-100kmph in just 3\.6 seconds which is as fast as a supercar\. This SUV can top out at a whopping speed of 305kmp/h making it the fastest SUV on sale today\. The transmission of this car is an eight\-speed \(Dual\-Clutch\) automatic sending power to all four wheels through Tamburo – Lamborghini’s dynamic control\.

Coming to the Interior of the car Lamborghini provides everything which people expect in an SUV\. The interior of the car is customizable and yet feels sporty and functional\. There are two digital screens on the centre console, while instrument cluster is all\-digital too\. The drive selector has 5 driving modes – Strada, Sport, Corsa, Sabia \(sand\), Terra \(land\), and Neve \(snow\)\. Of these, the last three are seen for the first time in a car from Sant ’Agata Bolognese\. Although the Urus is a proper four\-seater, the SUV can be had with a five\-seat configuration as well with 18\-way adjustable seats, ISOFIX, and sunroof\. The Urus has a 616 litres boot space which can be extended to 1,596 litres\.

Coming to the exterior of the car the Urus has every bit of supercar DNA with all the cutting\-edge design with all the lines and the aggressive face of the car\. The Urus gets 23\-inch wheels which is largest wheels ever fit to a production car and also it comes as standard with the world's largest set of carbon ceramic disc brakes for the Urus which is the largest ever brakes fitted to any production car in the world, with __440 mm \(17\.32 in\) discs in the front and 370 mm \(14\.57 in\) discs in the rear__ using ten\-piston callipers at the front and single\-piston callipers at the rear\.  

The 3 good things of the Urus are that 

- It has a new and nice\-looking design for the body\.
- It is also very practical for a Lamborghini\.
- The car has a supercar like performance even though it is a very practical SUV\.

The 3 bad things of the car are as follows

- It has a poor rear visibility
- The person sitting in the middle seat will feel a bit cramped because if the position of the middle seat\. \(Which is generally poor in almost every car\)
- The front seat headrests are slightly pushed forward which can be a bit annoying\.

The Final Verdict:

If you are looking for a Practical SUV which performs amazing and is combined with luxury then the Urus is a go to SUV which can please you while driving in the street as well as on fast motorways\.
