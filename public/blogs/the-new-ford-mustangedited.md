The New FORD mustang

Ford Mustang is an iconic supercar that was first started in the year 1969\. As petrolheads know the Mustang was named after a WWII fighter jet called “P\-51 Mustang”\. 

The first ford mustang was shipped to India in 2016\. Later over the years, many Mustangs have been imported to India\. This Iconic American Muscle car has been seen as a pony car in this part of the world\.

First off the Mustang starts with a base price of 71\.62 Lakhs for the base model and goes all the way up to 93\.51 Lakhs for the top\-end highly spec\-d car\.

Talking About the Performance and the Engine of the Mustang\-

The Mustang comes with a single\-engine variant in India which is a 5\.0L V8 engine that puts out 401 HP along with 515 NM of Torque at its peak\. This car gives 11\.8 km per liter which is OK ishh for a car in its class\. This car is driven by a 6\-speed\-select shift automated gearbox\. The Mustang can top out at 250 kmp/h which is quite impressive\.

How is the Mustang Like to Drive??

Straight away the New Mustang feels lovely to drive on open roads\. The 5\.0L V8 is quite smooth and the Automated Gearbox just feels perfect for longer journeys\. When you need to overtake someone, you just need to floor the throttle and the car just flies, the Gearbox in the car might feel a little slow in the comfort setting of the car as it takes a second or two to kick down but once it responds then the car is simply amazing\.  The car has 5 driving moods as Comfort/Normal, Wet, Track, Snow, or Sport\+ modes which can alter the throttle response, the suspension, the stability of the car, the steering weight\. This car gets an Adaptive Cruise Control system as well which can help in maintaining the speed\.

Infotainment System

The Ford Mustang comes with several features for occupants including HID headlights along with pony projection puddle lamps, power\-operated and retractable ORVMs, 8\-inch touch screen system, integrated audio system with CD player, Bluetooth, voice control, AM/FM back glass mounted antenna, trip meter and Ford SYNC2\.

Comfort and Interior of the Mustang…

Jumping into the interior of the car the Mustang fells just so comfortable in the inside\. The Leather seat covers do help the passengers to feel relaxed\. The cabin gets a full\-on single tone leather interior with various tech for the dash such as the touch screen, heated/cooling seats, the adaptive cruise control, and a powerful climate control system\.

The seats are 6\-way adjustable seats with heating/cooling attributes for the front seats\. The Windows are one\-touch power windows\. There are four\-way adjustable headrests with a 12V outlet, a locking center console, nice door bins for storing coffees/water bottles\. All\-in\-all the Interior of the mustang is very pleasing and is very comfortable for longer journeys\.

The Ford Mustang has a boot capacity of 383 liters which is pretty decent for a car in its segment\. The rear seats are 50:50 split\-folding seats which help to carry longer items\.

How safe is Ford Mustang?

Ford Mustang offers several safety features to owners including multiple airbags, seatbelt reminder system, Ford MyKey, ISOFIX child seat anchorage points, ABS, reverse sensing system, steering mounted cruise controls, reverse parking system, pull\-drift compensation, steering mounted cruise control, and front seatbelt pre\-tensioners\. Other features include the passive anti\-theft system, intelligent access, push\-button start, engine immobilizer, and perimetric alarm among others\.

Pros and Cons of the Mustang\.

- Excellent Driving Experience
- Modern technologies used
- Both on\-road and off\-road driving modes are available
- The back seat is too small for an adult to crawl into\.
- Interior quality leaves a lot to be desired
- A big car that can feel intimidating on narrow roads

Final Verdict on the New Mustang

If you are looking for an All\-American Sports Muscle car then we reckon that you should buy the Mustang, but wasting up all that money for a car with less luggage space and which is difficult for carrying the passengers at the back isn't worth it\.
