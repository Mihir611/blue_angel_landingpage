THE ALL\-NEW MERCEDES BENZ G63 //AMG

The All\-New 2021 Mercedes Benz G63 is a combination of both Luxury and Performance\. It is the Top of the model in the G\-Class segment and the price starts from around ₹2\.45 crore\. Yes, it is a Lavish car\.

But with a heavy price tag the vehicle comes excellent performance\. So, there is only 1 variant in the G63 AMG which is a 4\.0L Twin\-Turbo hand built V8 from AMG which puts out 585 Horsepower and 850 NM of Torque\. This engine max outs at 210 kmph, 0\-100kmph takes about 4\.2 seconds\. This engine is driven by a 9\-speed Automatic \(Torque Converter\) \-Gearbox with Paddle Shifters and Launch Control\. It also has an Off\-Road Mode and 3 Locking Differentials which helps in off\-roading\.  
  
So, What’s new about the “New G63”?

- It gets a new front grill compared to the old G63
- AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9G\.
- AMG Ride Control\.
- AMG high\-performance braking system\.
- MULTIBEAM LED – trend\-setting lighting technology\.
- Exclusive Interior\.
- AMG Performance steering wheel in nappe leather\.
- Glass sliding sunroof – more air, provides more light\.

It has individual front suspension with coil springs and has Rigid Axle at the back\. It has Ventilated Disc Brakes at the front\.

The fuel tank can hold 100 liters of fuel and the milage of the car is 6\.1kmpl and the estimated range on full tank is 610 kms\. Now being a Mercedes, this car has loads and loads of options to choose from to customize the vehicle\.  
  
All in all the New Mercedes\-Benz G63 AMG is a brilliant SUV to buy if one needs a performance SUV with lot of practicality\.
